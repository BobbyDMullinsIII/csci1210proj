About

Edge Gym located in Bristol, Tennessee is a local business dedicated to helping its members improve themselves physically. Whatever a member would like to accomplish, gaining weight, losing weight, becoming stronger; Edge Gym will make it happen. Our gym is equipped with state of the art machinery and weights as well as experienced and educated employees. Edge Gym welcomes all people and is a non-judgement zone where its members can feel comfortable. We offer a different variety of classes and instructors for our members to take advantage of.


Edge Gym of Bristol invites you to join our fitness family!
